About Mahindra & Mahindra


Founded in 1945, Mahindra & Mahindra is a major Indian multinational conglomerate with a prominent presence in the global automotive market. As a leading manufacturer of utility vehicles and tractors in India, Mahindra has built a reputation for ruggedness and reliability. Headquartered in Mumbai, Mahindra has diversified interests across various industries including automotive, agriculture, finance, hospitality, aerospace, and real estate.

Mahindra’s automotive sector is renowned for its range of SUVs, saloon cars, vans, commercial vehicles, and two-wheelers. Among its notable contributions to the automotive industry are its innovations in electric vehicles through its subsidiary, Mahindra Electric.

SThe company stands at the forefront of the Indian automobile market, known for models like the Scorpio, Bolero, XUV500, and Thar, which are popular for their durability and performance in diverse terrains. Mahindra’s tractor division, Mahindra & Mahindra Farm Equipment, is one of the largest in the world.

IWith a focus on sustainability and innovation, Mahindra continues to explore new frontiers in mobility and is committed to the ‘Rise’ philosophy that emphasizes socially and environmentally responsible business practices.

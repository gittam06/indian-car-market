About TATA Motors


Founded in 1945, TATA Motors is a part of the larger TATA Group, an Indian multinational conglomerate. It is one of India's leading automobile manufacturers and among the top in the commercial vehicle segment globally. With a strong presence across India and in international markets, TATA Motors has a diverse portfolio that includes a range of cars, sports utility vehicles, trucks, buses, and defense vehicles.

TATA Motors is committed to innovation that meets the needs of its customers. It has pioneered the development of fuel-efficient, environmentally friendly vehicles and is the company behind the TATA Nano, once dubbed the world's cheapest car. Additionally, TATA Motors acquired the prestigious brands Jaguar and Land Rover in 2008, further expanding its position in the global automotive industry.

The company has always been at the forefront of the Indian automotive market with a reputation for strength and durability. TATA Motors has also focused on sustainable and environmentally friendly manufacturing processes and contributes to the global shift towards cleaner, greener vehicles with its investments in electric vehicle technology.

With an eye towards the future, TATA Motors continues to innovate and adapt to the evolving needs of the market and its customers, committing to ‘Connecting aspirations’ as their philosophy, with a focus on quality, customer satisfaction, and innovation.

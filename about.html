About Indian Car Market Guide

The Indian Car Market Guide is your go-to source for the latest trends, reviews, and insights into the Indian automotive world. Launched in 2023 by Chitkara University Institute of Engineering and Technology, this platform is a project spearheaded by Gitam Pal with the vision to enlighten automobile enthusiasts and potential car buyers about the plethora of choices available in the Indian market.

Stay updated, make informed decisions, and drive the future with the Indian Car Market Guide.

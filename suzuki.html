About Maruti Suzuki


Founded in 1981, Maruti Suzuki India Limited is one of India's pioneering automobile manufacturing companies. A subsidiary of the Suzuki Motor Corporation of Japan, Maruti Suzuki has consistently brought innovations to the Indian car market. They have achieved numerous milestones and are recognized for their affordable cars, low maintenance, and widespread service network. With a mission to bring the joy of personal mobility to every household, Maruti Suzuki cars like Alto, Swift, Baleno, and Ciaz have become household names in India.
